kattakayam cherian mappillai ( 1859-1936 ) malayalam poet . hi magnum opu epic poem shreeyeshu vijayam compos span 15 year , finish year 1926 , earn titl mahakavi . He editor vijnaana rathnaakaram , one earli literari magazin ( start 1913 ) malayalam languag . He also co-found one earliest rubber trade compani kerala , call meenachil rubber compani . 
He hail kattakayam famili palai kottayam , kingdom travancor .
